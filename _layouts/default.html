Welcome to Coding Fest!


Coding Fest is a university-wide coding event organized by School of Computer Science, Faculty of Engineering to advocate a coding culture to students. It showcases interesting coding projects, connects like-minded thinkers, and engages industry with students.

Note that students from Faculty of Engineering may be able to claim PEP (Professional Engagement Program) hours for participating in Project Showcase and AWS DeepRacer Challenge.

Coding Fest 2021 will be held in a hybrid mode at 10am-2pm on Wed 9th June in the PNR (Peter Nicol Russel) Building at the University of Sydney. Free lunch is available.
