Welcome to the Coding Fest

Coding Fest is a university-wide coding event to advocate coding culture to students. It showcases interesting coding projects, connects like-minded thinkers, and engages with industry. In 2021, it is expected to have about 150 attendees from various disciplines such as engineering, science, business, architecture, and medicine and health. This would be a great opportunity for industry to engage with our students.
