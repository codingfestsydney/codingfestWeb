Expression of Interest Applications for Coding Fest 2021 (in a hybrid mode) are due on Friday 2nd April!

The School of Computer Science invites you to showcase your coding projects as part of Coding Fest 2021 on

Wed 9th June. All participants who are accepted into Project Showcase also have the chance to win one of several prizes (total value $2,500), so apply today!

  1. You will have until the end of the semester to develop your project. An application submission is to provide team information and project descriptions for the consideration of acceptance.
  2. Any coding project completed by yourself is welcome, such as assignment project, capstone project, Honours project, and personal project.
  3. All students with different levels of coding skills are welcome and there are awards for both junior and senior students.
  4. Online participation is welcome.

There are also opportunities to take part in the AWS DeepRacer Challenge and network with industry guests including Accenture, AWS and Microsoft.

Application deadline: Friday 2nd April
